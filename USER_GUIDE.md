# ROXREADY — User Guide

*A Hyrox training app that lives in a single file. No accounts, no subscriptions, your data never leaves your phone.*

> The same guide is built into the app — tap the **?** button on the Home screen.

---

## Getting started (2 minutes)

1. **Open `index.html`** in any browser (phone or computer — phone is best).
2. **Answer the onboarding questions** — goal, race date, division, training days, equipment, recent 5K. Be honest about days per week: consistency beats ambition.
3. **On a phone, install it**: browser menu → **"Add to Home Screen."** It gets an icon and opens full-screen like a real app.
4. Your **plan is generated automatically** from today to your race date.

⚠️ **Important:** your data is stored by the browser on that device. Don't clear the browser's site data without exporting a backup first (Settings → Export JSON).

## The weekly rhythm (how to actually use it)

| When | What | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Daily | Open the app, tap the energy check-in | Feeds the adaptive coach; 2 seconds |
| Training days | Do the session on Home → tap **Log this session** | Logging takes ~20s and powers everything else |
| Weekly | Glance at **Progress** | See split consistency + volume trending the right way |
| Every 4–6 weeks | Do the **simulation** in your plan | The report tells you exactly what to fix next |
| Monthly | Settings → **Export JSON** | Backup. Do it. |

## The five tabs

**🏠 Home** — today's session, race countdown, streak, energy check-in, and coach messages when the plan adapts.

**▤ Plan** — your periodized program:
- **Base** builds your engine (easy volume — boring on purpose)
- **Build** adds Hyrox loads and compromised (post-station) running
- **Specific** puts everything at race pace and race weights
- **Taper** is rest — the fitness is already banked
- **Deload** every 4th week is intentional: back off to absorb the work.

The plan **adapts by itself**: miss a key session → it's rescheduled within the week; two patchy weeks → the week trims ~25%; two great weeks that felt easy → it nudges up. Every change is explained in a coach message. You can also **edit any session, add your own, duplicate, or delete** — the plan is a scaffold, not a contract.

**＋ Log** — pick a type, fill what you have.
- **Run splits matter most.** Enter each 1 km split whenever you can — split evenness is the best predictor of a good Hyrox.
- Station times: log at **full race distance** when possible so comparisons stay honest. Your division's loads are shown everywhere.
- Always tap **RPE** (1 = stroll, 10 = collapse) — it drives the adaptive coach.
- Everything is editable/deletable later from History.

**Train at a fitness studio (HIIT / circuit / functional classes)?** Set how many classes a week during onboarding or in Settings. The plan then **reserves those days as class days and trims the overlapping conditioning**, so you're not doubling up. Log a class under the 🔥 **Studio Class** type — pick a focus (cardio / resistance / hybrid), quick-fill a common format, and optionally note the movements. You can **save your studio's regular workout as a reusable format** to log it in seconds next time. Classes count toward your streak and adherence like any other session.

**◔ Progress**
- **Split consistency (CV%)** — headline number. Under 5% = metronome. Over 9% = pacing or engine problem.
- **Radar** — strengths vs weaknesses against your goal pace, with a concrete "focus" suggestion.
- **Weekly volume** — build gradually (~10%/week max).
- **PRs & adherence** — 80%+ adherence is elite.

**⚑ Race** — set a goal time → per-km run targets and per-station targets. Go out **no faster** than the suggested opening kilometre: every second stolen early costs double at wall balls. After each simulation, open it in History for the full **report** (fade analysis, station vs target, Roxzone time).

## Streaks (designed to be humane)

- Counts **weeks**, not days. Goal = your training days **minus one**, so one busy day never ruins a week.
- Rest days never break a streak.
- One **grace week per month** is applied automatically.
- Broke anyway? No guilt mechanics — just start the next one.

## Sharing with friends

Send them the `index.html` file (WhatsApp, email, AirDrop, USB — anything). Each person gets their own onboarding, their own plan, and their own private data on their own device. Nothing is shared between phones.

## Backup / moving phones

Settings → **Export JSON** downloads everything. On the new device: open the app → Settings → **Import** → pick the file. Done.

## FAQ

- **No reminder notification?** Browser notifications only fire while the app is open (there's no server). Set a phone alarm for your training days as backup.
- **Race date or schedule changed?** Update Settings, then Plan → **Regenerate**.
- **Weights are in kg?** Yes — official Hyrox standards are metric (season 25/26 standards are built in).
- **I train my own way.** Skip the plan and just log — streaks, PRs, radar and sim reports all work from your logs alone.
- **Is it medical advice?** No. Train sensibly, respect pain, see a professional for injuries.
